Our Leader in Me Programme

The Leader in Me is a whole-school transformation model that empowers students with the leadership and life skills they need to thrive in the 21st century.

Based on Stephen Covey's The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, this program helps students develop skills like responsibility, goal-setting, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Vistara Learning Centre

The Vistara Learning Centre (VLC) is a unique educational environment where every child's potential for self-directed learning is nurtured. We believe in fostering intrinsic motivation, curiosity, and self-discipline that extends far beyond the classroom.

About VLC

The Vistara Learning Centre is a thoughtfully designed educational space that promotes independent, accelerated, and passion-driven learning. The primary goal of VLC is to nurture students into self-directed learners who manage their time effectively, pursue academic excellence, and explore diverse interests.
